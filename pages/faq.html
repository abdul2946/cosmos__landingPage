FAQ

Common Questions and Troubleshooting Tips:

1. What should I do if my my internet connection drops?

  • Reconnect Quickly: Try to reconnect as soon as possible. Your progress should be saved, allowing you to continue from where you left off.
  • Contact Support: If you face persistent issues, contact support immediately.

2. Can I take a break during the assessment?

  • Reconnect Quickly: Try to reconnect as soon as possible. Your progress should be saved, allowing you to continue from where you left off.
  • Contact Support: If you face persistent issues, contact support immediately.

3. What if I encounter technical issue?

  • Check Connection: Verify your internet connection is stable.
  • Browser Settings: Ensure your browser is up to date and that your camera and microphone are enabled.
  • Help Center: Visit the Help Center for detailed troubleshooting tips.
  • Support: Contact our support team for assistance.

5. Can I go back to previous aquestion?

  • One-Way Navigation: Once you move to the next question, you cannot return to previous questions. Ensure you are satisfied with your answer before proceeding.

6. How long does section of the assessment take?

  • Section Timings: The duration of each section will be displayed before you start. Manage your time effectively to complete all questions.

7. What should I do if I miss a scheduled assessment?

  • Reschedule: Check if there is an option to reschedule or contact your recruiter for further instructions.

8. How do I know my assessment is submitted?

  • Confirmation: After clicking "Submit," a confirmation message will appear indicating successful submission.
